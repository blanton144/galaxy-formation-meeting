The meeting will be held at Biosphere 2, about 45 minutes north of Tucson, Arizona.

Biosphere 2 has on site housing, which can accommodate about half of our anticipated attendance, and for the other half we will arrange a nearby hotel block, with shuttles available for transportation to Biosphere 2.

Breakfast, lunch, and dinner will be available on site. There are basically no other practical options for meals.
